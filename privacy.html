Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 11, 2025 • Effective date: October 11, 2025

Adetop Tech Services Limited ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy and personal data. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, how we use it, and the choices you have.

1. Information we collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us and information we collect automatically when you use our services.

  • Account & contact data: name, email, phone, company name, billing information when you contact us or sign up for services.
  • Communications: messages, support requests, and other correspondence.
  • Usage data: pages visited, features used, IP address, browser and device information, cookies and similar technologies.
  • Payment data: transaction and invoice data required for billing; payment processing is handled by third-party providers and tokens only are stored when necessary.

2. How we use your data

We use data to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our services.
  • Communicate with you about your account and provide customer support.
  • Process payments and prevent fraud.
  • Personalize features and content, and analyze usage to improve our products.
  • Meet legal and regulatory obligations.

3. Legal bases for processing (EU/GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area, we process personal data under the following legal bases:

  • Contractual necessity: to perform a contract or provide requested services.
  • Legitimate interests: for security, fraud prevention, and improving services (we balance these against your rights and freedoms).
  • Consent: for optional marketing communications and cookies where we ask for consent.
  • Legal obligation: when required to comply with the law.

4. Cookies and similar technologies

We and our partners use cookies, local storage, and similar technologies to provide functionality, analyze usage, and deliver personalized content. You can control cookies via your browser settings. Essential cookies required for our service operation are used by default.

5. Sharing your information

We may share data with:

  • Service providers that perform services on our behalf (e.g., hosting, analytics, payment processors).
  • Legal authorities when required by law or to protect our rights and safety.
  • Affiliates or in connection with a business transfer (e.g., merger, sale of assets) — with notice and contract protections.

6. Third-party services

We use third-party services for analytics, hosting, email, and payments. These parties have their own privacy policies and may collect data in accordance with those policies. We require them to process data in accordance with contractual instructions and applicable law.

7. Data retention

We retain personal data as long as necessary to provide services, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements. Retention periods vary depending on the type of data and the purpose for which it was collected.

8. Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect data from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. No system is completely secure — if we learn of a breach that may pose risk to individuals, we will notify affected parties and authorities as required by law.

9. Your rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights including:

  • Access and receive a copy of your personal data.
  • Rectify inaccurate data.
  • Request erasure of data (right to be forgotten) where applicable.
  • Restrict or object to processing.
  • Data portability — receive data in a structured, commonly used format.
  • Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent.

To exercise these rights, contact us using the details below. We may need to verify your identity before fulfilling requests.

10. Children

Our services are not intended for children under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe we collected such data, contact us to have it removed.

11. International transfers

We are based in Nigeria and may transfer data to countries with different data protection laws. We use legal safeguards (e.g., Standard Contractual Clauses) and appropriate measures to protect transfers.

12. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy. If we make significant changes, we will provide notice by posting a notice on our website or by other means. The "Last updated" date at the top shows when it was last changed.
